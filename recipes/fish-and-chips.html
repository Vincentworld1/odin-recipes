Fish and Chips

Description

fish covered with batter (= a mixture of flour, eggs and milk) and then fried and served with pieces of fried potato, often eaten in the UK as a takeaway food (= food bought at a shop or restaurant and taken home or somewhere else to eat): We took the fish and chips home and ate them straight from the paper.

Ingredients

  • Fish Fillets
  • Potatoes
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Beer or Water
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the Fish: Season the fish fillets with salt and pepper, then dip them in flour to coat lightly.
  2. Make the Batter: In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, cold beer or water, and a pinch of salt until smooth. The batter should be thick enough to coat the fish.
  3. Prepare the Chips: Peel and cut potatoes into thick strips. Soak them in cold water for 30 minutes, then pat dry.
  4. Fry the Fish and Chips: Heat oil in a deep fryer or a pan. Dip the fish into the batter and fry until golden brown. Fry the chips until crispy.
  5. Serve and Enjoy: Drain excess oil from both the fish and chips, then serve hot with tartar sauce or malt vinegar.
